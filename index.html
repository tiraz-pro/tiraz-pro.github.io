Engineering Precision Meets Aesthetic Vision

At TIRAZ, we are more than just contractors. We approach every project with a strict engineering mindset, ensuring that aesthetic designs are perfectly translated into reality.

We believe that flawless execution starts with precise Shop Drawings and accurate Bill of Quantities (BOQ). Our process guarantees zero material waste, strict budget adherence, and unparalleled finishing quality.

Whether you have a ready design or need a complete turnkey solution, we provide:

  • Accurate BOQ: Detailed quantity surveying to prevent budget overruns.
  • Shop Drawings: Resolving design conflicts before execution begins.
  • On-Site Supervision: Daily management to ensure execution matches standards.
